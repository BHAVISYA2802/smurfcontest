Welcome to the Smurf Contest

The Smurf Contest is an international festival that celebrates creativity, community, and the joy of Smurfs. Since 2020, our event has grown into a luxurious, magical experience for fans of all ages. Join us as we bring art, stories, and performances to life in a world where blue dreams come true

A Message from Papa Smurf

Dear Smurfs and friends, We started this contest with a simple idea: to inspire creativity and unity in a magical setting. Every year, I am amazed by the incredible talent, passion, and friendship this event sparks. Together, we make the Smurf world shine brighter than ever. Let’s keep smurfing forward.

– Papa Smurf–

Our Journey So Far

2021: Added Art and Costume Categories

2022: Introduced Virtual Events and Live Streams

2023: International entries from 12+ countries

2024: Record-breaking participation with 500+ Smurf fans
